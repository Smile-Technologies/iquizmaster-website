Create & Edit Quizzes

Create and edit interactive and fun quizzes with IQuizmaster in accordance with your class.

Schedule and Share

Schedule the quizzes that are created and share them with one student or an entire class at anychosen time.Get quizzin' anytime!

Collaborate

Iquizmaster enables the collaboration of one or more teachers at a time to create a particular quiz. Put together a quiz that challenges and helps the students grow with IQuizmaster.

Audio

Give an illusion of a classroom by adding audio questions, this feature makes it more authentic and easier to understand.

Variety of questions

Quizzes are fun when there are a variety of questions, put together an interactive quiz session with fill in the blanks, match the following, MCQ's etc. Let's get busy quizzing!

Results and valuations

Instant evaluations that aid in understanding the students better and these analytics enables easy assessment of the class's learning progress.

Video sharing

Facilitates sharing of youtube videos before or after quizzes to give a better insight to the students.

Unique codes for access

Each quiz is encrypted with a unique code that allows the students to access the quiz. Every quiz can be accessed with these codes only.

Flash Card session

Make the process of learning fun with flashcard sessions, an alternate and chosen way that helps in reinforcing and improving knowledge retention.

Gamification

Make your sessions fun and keep your students comingfor more with rewards. Iquizmaster allows rewarding students after each session with points and badges.
